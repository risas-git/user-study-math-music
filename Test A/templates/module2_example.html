Module 2 Reference & Worked Examples

Example 1: Polynomial Derivatives & Power Rule

Find the derivative of $y = 3x^4 + 5x^2 - 7x + 2$

Step 1 (Apply Power Rule): $\frac{d}{dx}(3x^4) = 12x^3$, $\frac{d}{dx}(5x^2) = 10x$, $\frac{d}{dx}(-7x) = -7$
Step 2 (Constant Derivative): $\frac{d}{dx}(2) = 0$
Answer: $y' = 12x^3 + 10x - 7$

Example 2: Definite Integration & Fundamental Theorem

Evaluate $\int_1^3 (3x^2 - 2x) \, dx$

Step 1 (Find Antiderivative): $F(x) = \int (3x^2 - 2x) dx = x^3 - x^2$
Step 2 (Evaluate Limits $F(3) - F(1)$): $(3^3 - 3^2) - (1^3 - 1^2) = (27 - 9) - (1 - 1) = 18 - 0 = 18$
Answer: $18$

Example 3: Product Rule & Logarithms

Find the derivative of $y = x^3 \cdot \ln(x)$

Step 1 (Identify $u$ and $v$): $u = x^3 \Rightarrow u' = 3x^2$ and $v = \ln(x) \Rightarrow v' = \frac{1}{x}$
Step 2 (Apply $u'v + uv'$): $3x^2 \ln(x) + x^3 \cdot \frac{1}{x} = 3x^2 \ln(x) + x^2 = x^2(3\ln(x) + 1)$
Answer: $y' = x^2(3\ln(x) + 1)$

Example 4: Integration by Parts

Evaluate $\int x e^x \, dx$

Step 1 (Set $u$ and $dv$): $u = x \Rightarrow du = dx$ and $dv = e^x dx \Rightarrow v = e^x$
Step 2 (Apply $uv - \int v du$): $x e^x - \int e^x dx = x e^x - e^x + C = e^x(x - 1) + C$
Answer: $e^x(x - 1) + C$
