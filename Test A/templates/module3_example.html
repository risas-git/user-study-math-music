Module 3 Reference & Worked Examples

Example 1: Linear Equation Isolation

Solve for $x$: $4x - 7 = 17$

Step 1 (Add constant 7 to both sides): $4x = 17 + 7 = 24$
Step 2 (Divide by coefficient 4): $x = \frac{24}{4} = 6$
Answer: $x = 6$

Example 2: Quadratic Equation Factoring

Solve for $x$: $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$

Step 1 (Find factors of +6 that sum to -5): $-2$ and $-3$
Step 2 (Factor quadratic): $(x - 2)(x - 3) = 0$
Step 3 (Solve linear roots): $x - 2 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 2$ and $x - 3 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 3$
Answer: $x = 2, 3$

Example 3: Quadratic Formula & Discriminant Analysis

Solve for $x$: $2x^2 + 7x + 3 = 0$

Step 1 (Identify $a, b, c$): $a = 2, b = 7, c = 3$
Step 2 (Calculate Discriminant $D$): $D = b^2 - 4ac = 7^2 - 4(2)(3) = 49 - 24 = 25 > 0$ (2 real distinct rational roots)
Step 3 (Apply Formula): $x = \frac{-7 \pm \sqrt{25}}{2(2)} = \frac{-7 \pm 5}{4} \Rightarrow x = -\frac{2}{4} = -\frac{1}{2}$ or $x = -\frac{12}{4} = -3$
Answer: $x = -\frac{1}{2}, -3$

Example 4: Non-Linear Systems of Equations

Solve the system: $x + y = 10$ and $xy = 21$

Step 1 (Substitute $y$): $y = 10 - x \rightarrow x(10 - x) = 21 \rightarrow 10x - x^2 = 21$
Step 2 (Rearrange into standard quadratic): $x^2 - 10x + 21 = 0 \rightarrow (x - 7)(x - 3) = 0$
Step 3 (Find $(x,y)$ pairs): When $x = 7 \Rightarrow y = 3$; when $x = 3 \Rightarrow y = 7$
Answer: $(x,y) = (7,3)$ or $(3,7)$
