Module 3 Reference & Worked Examples

Example 1: Linear Equation Isolation

Solve for $x$: $5x - 8 = 22$

Step 1 (Add constant 8 to both sides): $5x = 22 + 8 = 30$
Step 2 (Divide by coefficient 5): $x = \frac{30}{5} = 6$
Answer: $x = 6$

Example 2: Quadratic Equation Factoring

Solve for $x$: $x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0$

Step 1 (Find factors of +12 that sum to -7): $-3$ and $-4$
Step 2 (Factor quadratic): $(x - 3)(x - 4) = 0$
Step 3 (Solve linear roots): $x - 3 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 3$ and $x - 4 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 4$
Answer: $x = 3, 4$

Example 3: Quadratic Formula & Discriminant Analysis

Solve for $x$: $2x^2 + 9x + 4 = 0$

Step 1 (Identify $a, b, c$): $a = 2, b = 9, c = 4$
Step 2 (Calculate Discriminant $D$): $D = b^2 - 4ac = 9^2 - 4(2)(4) = 81 - 32 = 49 > 0$ (2 real distinct rational roots)
Step 3 (Apply Formula): $x = \frac{-9 \pm \sqrt{49}}{2(2)} = \frac{-9 \pm 7}{4} \Rightarrow x = -\frac{2}{4} = -\frac{1}{2}$ or $x = -\frac{16}{4} = -4$
Answer: $x = -\frac{1}{2}, -4$

Example 4: Non-Linear Systems of Equations

Solve the system: $x + y = 12$ and $xy = 35$

Step 1 (Substitute $y$): $y = 12 - x \rightarrow x(12 - x) = 35 \rightarrow 12x - x^2 = 35$
Step 2 (Rearrange into standard quadratic): $x^2 - 12x + 35 = 0 \rightarrow (x - 7)(x - 5) = 0$
Step 3 (Find $(x,y)$ pairs): When $x = 7 \Rightarrow y = 5$; when $x = 5 \Rightarrow y = 7$
Answer: $(x,y) = (7,5)$ or $(5,7)$
