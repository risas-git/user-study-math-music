Module 2: Differentiation & Integration

This module tests fundamental and advanced differential and integral calculus concepts. Review the rules below before starting the exercise.

1. Power Rule for Derivatives & Integrals

• Derivative: $\frac{d}{dx}(x^n) = n x^{n-1}$ (Derivative of a constant $c$ is $0$).
• Indefinite Integral: $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1)$.

2. Exponential, Trigonometric & Logarithmic Calculus

• $\frac{d}{dx}(e^{kx}) = k e^{kx} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{d}{dx}(\sin(kx)) = k \cos(kx)$
• $\frac{d}{dx}\ln(g(x)) = \frac{g'(x)}{g(x)}$

3. Product & Quotient Rules

• Product Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}(u \cdot v) = u'v + uv'$
• Quotient Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{u}{v}\right) = \frac{u'v - uv'}{v^2}$

4. Integration by Substitution & Parts

• $u$-substitution: Substitute $u = g(x) \Rightarrow du = g'(x)dx$.
• Integration by Parts: $\int u \, dv = uv - \int v \, du$.
